Site Name: Amazon Sortation Center - MSP8

This name represents a business within Amazon's middle mile network located in Woodbury, MN. Sortation is the primary process used to move customer orders through Amazon's middle mile portion of its supply chain

Site Purpose: For internal Amazonians to learn more information about MSP8

The site includes information about material handling equipment at the site and how it moves packages through Amazon's supply chain. It will also give a history of the site's launch and forward-looking plans. The site will also contain an org chart of its currently leadership structure.

Scenarios: For those Amazonians interested in learning more about MSP8

  • What type of building is MSP8?
  • What type of equipment does MSP8 use to process packages?
  • Who are the current leaders of MSP8?
  • What are the long-terms plans for MSP8?
  • When did MSP8 launch and what did it's "ramp up" look like?
